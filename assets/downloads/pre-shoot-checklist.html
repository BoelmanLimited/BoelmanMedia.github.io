Pre-Shoot Checklist

Essential Steps to Prepare Your Property for Professional Photography

EXTERIOR

  • Remove vehicles from driveway
  • Store garbage bins out of sight
  • Clear walkways and driveway of debris
  • Remove seasonal decorations
  • Clean windows and screens
  • Mow lawn and trim edges
  • Remove garden tools and hoses
  • Turn on landscape lighting (if applicable)
  • Clean outdoor furniture
  • Remove pool equipment and covers
  • Clear leaves and debris from yard
  • Store children's toys
  • Remove visible water hoses
  • Clean gutters of visible debris

INTERIOR - GENERAL

  • Turn on ALL lights and lamps
  • Replace any burnt-out bulbs
  • Open all blinds/curtains fully
  • Remove personal photos
  • Clear countertops of clutter
  • Empty all trash bins
  • Hide power cords where possible
  • Clean all mirrors and glass surfaces
  • Vacuum/sweep all floors
  • Remove seasonal decorations
  • Hide pet items (beds, bowls, toys)
  • Make all beds neatly
  • Fluff and arrange all pillows
  • Remove remote controls
  • Hide children's toys
  • Store away personal items

KITCHEN

  • Clear countertops (leave max 2-3 appliances)
  • Remove magnets/papers from refrigerator
  • Clean all appliances until they shine
  • Remove dishes from sink
  • Hide dish soap, sponges, towels
  • Tuck away trash bins
  • Remove floor mats
  • Clear windowsills
  • Hide paper towels
  • Remove items from top of refrigerator
  • Clean inside of microwave
  • Organize visible pantry items

BATHROOMS

  • Clear countertops completely
  • Remove ALL personal hygiene items
  • Clean mirrors and glass thoroughly
  • Remove toilet items and plunger
  • Hide trash bins
  • Remove used towels
  • Close toilet lids
  • Remove bath mats
  • Remove shower curtains (if stained)
  • Clear items from shower/tub
  • Remove cleaning supplies
  • Store away personal robes

BEDROOMS

  • Make beds with fresh, wrinkle-free linens
  • Clear nightstands of personal items
  • Remove family photos
  • Store away clothing and shoes
  • Clear floors of all items
  • Organize closets (if to be photographed)
  • Remove exercise equipment
  • Hide phone chargers and cables
  • Remove personal items from walls
  • Clean under beds (if visible)

FINAL CHECKS

  • All lights on and working
  • All doors open (except closets)
  • Blinds/curtains open and even
  • Personal items secured
  • Property access instructions ready
  • Alarm codes available (if needed)
  • Pets secured or removed
  • HVAC set to comfortable temperature
  • All rooms decluttered
  • Windows clean inside and out
  • Fresh air flowing through house
  • All ceiling fans off
Pro Tip: Complete this checklist the day before your photo session to ensure everything is ready. A well-prepared property photographs beautifully and makes a stronger impression on potential buyers.
