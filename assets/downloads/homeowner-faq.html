Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know About Real Estate Photography

BEFORE THE SHOOT

How long should I plan for the photo session?

A typical photo session takes 1-2 hours, depending on your home's size. Larger homes or additional services like twilight photos or video may require more time.

Do I need to be present during the shoot?

While not required, it's helpful to be available at the start of the session to:

  • Provide access to all areas
  • Point out special features
  • Address any last-minute concerns
What if it's raining on photo day?

We monitor weather conditions and will contact you if rescheduling is recommended. Exterior photos are best taken on dry days with good lighting conditions.

DURING THE SHOOT

Should I leave lights on or off?

Please turn ON all interior lights, including:

  • Ceiling lights and lamps
  • Under-cabinet lighting
  • Accent lighting

We'll adjust as needed during the shoot.

What about pets during the photo session?

We recommend securing pets in a garage, basement, or off-site during the shoot. This ensures their safety and prevents them from appearing in photos.

Can you photograph specific features or angles?

Absolutely! Let us know about any special features or preferred angles at the start of the session. We aim to highlight your home's best attributes.

AFTER THE SHOOT

When will I receive my photos?

You'll receive your professionally edited photos within 24 hours through our online gallery system. Rush delivery is available upon request.

What if I need revisions?

We offer complimentary basic revisions within 48 hours of delivery. Simply let us know what adjustments you need, and we'll take care of it promptly.

How do I download and share the photos?

Your online gallery includes options to:

  • Download individual photos
  • Download the entire gallery
  • Share directly to social media
  • Share via email link
Pro Tip: For the best results, review our Pre-Shoot Checklist and Home Staging Guide before your session. Well-prepared homes photograph beautifully and typically sell faster!
