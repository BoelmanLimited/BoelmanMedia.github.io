Booking Guide

How to Schedule Your Professional Real Estate Photography Session

BEFORE BOOKING

1. Property Preparation

Ensure the property will be ready for photography:

  • Property is clean and staged
  • All maintenance and repairs are complete
  • Landscaping is finished
  • Download our Pre-Shoot Checklist for detailed preparation steps

BOOKING PROCESS

2. Schedule Your Session

Choose your preferred booking method:

  • Online booking through our website
  • Phone: (515) XXX-XXXX
  • Email: contact@boelmanmedia.com
3. Select Your Package

Choose the services you need:

  • Standard Photo Package
  • Twilight Photography
  • Aerial Photography
  • Virtual Tours
  • Video Tours
4. Provide Property Details
  • Property address
  • Square footage
  • Number of rooms
  • Special features to highlight
  • Access instructions

WHAT TO EXPECT

5. Day of Photography
  • We arrive at the scheduled time
  • Session typically takes 1-2 hours
  • We photograph all requested areas
  • Property should be photo-ready upon arrival
6. Delivery & Review
  • Photos delivered within 24 hours
  • Online gallery for easy access
  • Free touch-ups if needed
  • Download in high resolution
Note: For twilight sessions, flexible scheduling may be required based on sunset times and weather conditions.
